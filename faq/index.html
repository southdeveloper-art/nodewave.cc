Lavalink is a standalone audio sending node written in Java that handles the audio processing, decoding, and socket streams for Discord music bots. By hosting Lavalink on dedicated high-performance hardware, your bot code does not have to execute complex media parsing, preventing sound lag, voice cracking, and CPU bottlenecks on your main bot container.

Deployment is 100% automated and instant. As soon as invoice verification is complete, your Pterodactyl container login credentials are emailed to you, allowing you to upload your files, manage dependencies, and boot your server immediately.
